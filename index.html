Frequently Asked Questions

Crucial guidelines for your United States study plans.

Do I absolutely need IELTS/TOEFL to study in the USA?
No. Many of our partner universities accept a Medium of Instruction (MOI) certificate from your previous school/university as proof of English proficiency.
What are the initial university deposit requirements?
We secure admissions in programs with $0 initial tuition deposits before departure. Your academic tuition is paid locally in the USA after landing.
Can I travel with my spouse and children?
Yes, through the F2 visa path. We assist you through simultaneous or subsequent dependent filing with full visa interview training for both partners.
