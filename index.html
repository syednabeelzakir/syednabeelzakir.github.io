Top Innovation Hub

United States

Access world-class research institutions, unmatched academic flexibility, and robust OPT career pathways.

  • Competitive GPA (Minimum 3.0 recommended for top universities).
  • Standardized Tests: SAT/ACT for Undergraduates; GRE/GMAT for Graduates.
  • English Proficiency: TOEFL iBT (80+) or IELTS Academic (6.5+).
  • Strong Statement of Purpose (SOP) and 2-3 Letters of Recommendation (LOR).
  • Receive Form I-20 from SEVP-certified institution.
  • Pay SEVIS I-901 fee and complete DS-160 online visa application.
  • Schedule and clear the F-1 visa interview at the US Embassy/Consulate.
  • Demonstrate liquid financial funds covering tuition and living expenses for the first year.

Undergraduate (Bachelor's) $20,000 – $55,000 / year
Postgraduate (Master's/Ph.D.) $25,000 – $60,000 / year

High Immigration Pathways

Canada

Experience high-standard education, post-graduation work permits (PGWP), and a welcoming multicultural environment.

  • High School transcripts with solid grades for Bachelor programs.
  • Four-year Bachelor's degree equivalent for Master’s programs.
  • English Proficiency: IELTS Academic overall score of 6.5 (no band less than 6.0) or TOEFL equivalent.
  • Updated Resume and detailed academic Statement of Purpose.
  • Letter of Acceptance (LoA) from a designated learning institution (DLI).
  • Student Direct Stream (SDS) option available for faster processing with valid IELTS and GIC.
  • Proof of sufficient funds (Tuition + CAD 10,000+ for living expenses).
  • Biometrics and medical examination clearance.
Study Level Average Annual Tuition
Undergraduate (Bachelor's) CAD 18,000 – CAD 36,000 / year
Postgraduate (Master's) CAD 17,000 – CAD 35,000 / year
